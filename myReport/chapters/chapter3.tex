\textbf{Domain and organisations} \newline
93 customer service handles of organisations were identified across 9 domains. Since an organisation could have business activities across domains, the assigned domain was based on the products or services receiving the most number of complaints. The data was extracted via the Twitter API \footnote{https://developer.twitter.com/en}
